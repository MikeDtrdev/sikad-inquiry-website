How to Use Sikad

Get started with our bike rental system in just a few simple steps

1

Download & Register

Download the Sikad app and create your account with your personal information and contact details.

2

Scan QR Code

Locate an available bike and scan the QR code on the bike to initiate the rental process.

3

Make Payment

Complete payment through GCash integration for a secure and convenient transaction.

4

Unlock & Ride

Scan the generated QR code on the bike's display to unlock and start your ride within the designated area.

5

Return & Park

Return the bike to any designated parking area within the geofenced zone and end your rental session.

6

Rent Again

Ready for your next adventure? Simply repeat the process to rent another bike anytime you need it.

Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines for a secure and enjoyable ride

Wear Safety Gear

Always wear a helmet and appropriate safety gear when riding. Safety first!

Stay in Designated Areas

Keep within the geofenced zones to avoid automatic braking and alerts.

Follow Traffic Rules

Obey all traffic signals, signs, and local traffic regulations while riding.

Monitor Rental Time

Keep track of your rental duration to avoid overtime charges and ensure bike availability.

Report Issues

Report any bike malfunctions, accidents, or safety concerns through the app immediately.

Check Weather Conditions

Be aware of weather conditions and avoid riding in severe weather or poor visibility.
